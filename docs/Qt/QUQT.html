Introduction

    The main forum for Qt support and resourcing can be found at: https://forum.qt.io/ .

    Just as a warning - one can get one's head bitten off (and quite ferrociously) posting "newbie" or "repetitive" questions on these high-level forums.

    Research the problem EXTENSIVELY and use the KISS (Keep it Simple for the Stupid) principle if posting on the high-level forums. Do not take any stupid or antisocial comments that you may receive back to heart. Remeber some people on those forums never see daylight ...

    Users of the JTSDK Forum are by and large very helpful and considerate in responses. Have no hesitation asking questions in our place first. We have some of the best, finest and real-word considerate programmers on the planet as part of our community.
